# ðŸ”µ 5. FUNDING QUESTIONS (DTPs, AHRC/ESRC, ETC.)

### **How does funding work for UK PhDs?**
Funding comes from:

1. **DTPs (Doctoral Training Partnerships)**  
2. **University scholarships**  
3. **Supervisor grants (STEM)**  
4. **External fellowships**  
5. **Self-funding**

### **What is a DTP?**
A consortium of universities offering PhD funding in specific fields.

Examples:

- AHRC for arts/humanities  
- ESRC for social sciences  
- BBSRC, MRC, NERC for STEM  
- EPSRC for engineering & physical sciences  

DTPs offer:

- stipend  
- tuition coverage  
- research training  
- cohort-based learning

---

### **How competitive is DTP funding?**
Very competitive â€” often comparable to elite U.S. programs.

- strong supervisor backing  
- a polished proposal  
- excellent references  
- good interview performance

---

### **Can international students get UK funding?**
Yes â€” but places are limited.

Some DTPs cap international students at **30%** of funded slots.

---

### **Can I apply for a PhD first and worry about funding later?**
Not usually. Many funding decisions happen **at the same time** as program decisions.
