Funding in the UK is NOT automatic for international students.

Funding may come from:
- **UKRI Research Councils** (ESRC, AHRC, EPSRC, MRC, etc.)
- **University scholarships** (Clarendon, Gates Cambridge, Imperial Presidentâ€™s PhD Award)
- **Supervisor-funded projects** (via grants)

- Not all departments can fund international students  
- Funding deadlines often come **before** the main application deadline  

Funding is the biggest barrier for international applicants.
