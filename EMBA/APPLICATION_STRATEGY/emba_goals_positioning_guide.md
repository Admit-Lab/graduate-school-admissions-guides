# EMBA Career Goals & Positioning Guide

Your EMBA goals must be:
- **Specific**  
- **Realistic**  
- **Organizationally grounded**  
- **Timeline-driven**  

Avoid MBA-style early-career goals.  
EMBA committees expect clarity and evidence of organizational context.
