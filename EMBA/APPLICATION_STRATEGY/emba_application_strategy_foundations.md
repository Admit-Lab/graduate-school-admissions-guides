# EMBA Application Strategy Foundations  
A Guide by Dr. Philippe Barr, Founder of The Admit Lab

An Executive MBA (EMBA) is not a career-starter degree.  
It is a **career-accelerator** for professionals with 10+ years of experience and demonstrated leadership impact.

Successful EMBA applications show:
- **Organizational influence**, not tasks  
- **Leadership maturity**, not ambition alone  
- **Strategic thinking**, not technical competence  
- **Clarity of purpose**, not vague career aspirations  
- **Readiness for academic rigor while working full-time**

This guide outlines the foundational elements of a competitive EMBA application strategy.
