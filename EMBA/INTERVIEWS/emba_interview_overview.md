# EMBA Interview Overview  
A Comprehensive Guide for Executive MBA Applicants  
By Dr. Philippe Barr, Founder of The Admit Lab

EMBA interviews are fundamentally different from interviews for early-career programs.

They are not checking:
- your future potential  
- your technical skills  
- your youthful ambition  

They are evaluating:
- **executive maturity**
- **judgment**
- **clarity of purpose**
- **organizational impact**
- **readiness for the workload**
