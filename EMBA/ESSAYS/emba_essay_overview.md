- time management  
- delegation  
- prioritization systems  
- support structures  

This determines whether you will thrive or withdraw.

---

## **5. Can you contribute meaningfully to a senior cohort?**
EMBA cohorts learn heavily from each other.

Committees seek candidates who will:
- elevate classroom discussions  
- share insight without dominating  
- collaborate with peers across industries  
- respect diversity of senior experience  

---

## **6. Do you understand what this *specific* EMBA program offers?**
You must show:
- program philosophy fit  
- leadership labs or coaching alignment  
- understanding of curriculum depth  
- faculty or resources relevant to your goals  

This is where most weak essays fail.

---

## **7. Do you write with clarity, concision, executive presence, and depth?**
The writing itself is a demonstration of:
- communication skill  
- analytical ability  
- maturity  
- precision of thought  

If your writing feels immature or rambling, the committee assumes your leadership is too.
