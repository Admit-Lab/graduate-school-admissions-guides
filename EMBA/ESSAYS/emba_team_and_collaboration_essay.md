# EMBA Team & Collaboration Essay Guide  
How to Show You Are Ready for a Cohort-Based Executive Program

Most EMBA programs ask a question about teamwork or collaboration because the cohort structure is central to the experience.

Committees want to know:
- How you work with senior peers  
- How you handle conflict  
- How you communicate upwards and across organizations  
- Whether you elevate teams rather than dominate them
