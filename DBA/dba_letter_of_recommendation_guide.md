# DBA Letters of Recommendation Guide  
What Strong Referees Must Demonstrate

A strong DBA letter must discuss:

1. Leadership capability  
2. Strategic insight  
3. Ability to diagnose organizational problems  
4. Writing and communication skill  
5. Intellectual maturity  
6. Capacity to complete a doctoral-level project  

Unlike PhD letters, DBA letters may come from:
- CEOs  
- Direct supervisors  
- Cross-functional partners  
- Clients or board members  

Academic letters are optional unless your academic record is old or weak.
