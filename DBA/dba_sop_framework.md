# DBA Statement of Purpose Framework  
A Guide by Dr. Philippe Barr, Founder of The Admit Lab

The DBA Statement of Purpose is not an academic PhD proposal and not an MBA career essay.  
It is a **professional research justification** that must demonstrate:

1. Your real-world business problem  
2. The applied research gap you intend to address  
3. How your industry background equips you to study this problem  
4. Why a *DBA* (not a PhD or MBA) is the correct pathway  
5. Why the specific programâ€™s faculty, structure, and methodology fit your goals
