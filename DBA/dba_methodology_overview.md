# DBA Methodology Overview  
What Committees Expect You To Understand (At a Basic Level)

You do NOT need a full research design to apply to a DBA.  
However, you DO need to understand which methodologies align with applied business problems.

### **1. Case Study Research**
Ideal for organizational change or leadership problems.

### **2. Action Research**
Conducting iterative cycles of intervention and evaluation inside your organization.

### **3. Mixed Methods**
Combining qualitative and quantitative data to understand complex systems.

### **4. Program Evaluation**
Assessing impact of organizational initiatives.

### **5. Survey-Based Research**
Useful for culture, leadership, customer experience, or HR analytics problems.
